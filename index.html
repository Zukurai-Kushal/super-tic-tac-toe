What is Super Tic Tac Toe?

Super tic-tac-toe (also known as ultimate tic-tac-toe, meta tic-tac-toe or (tic-tac-toe)²) is a board game composed of nine tic-tac-toe boards arranged in a 3 × 3 grid. Players take turns playing on the smaller tic-tac-toe boards until one of them wins on the larger board.
